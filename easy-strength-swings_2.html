The same 5 exercises for all 40 sessions. Load changes. Exercises don't. That's the point — make heavy feel easy through repetition, not variety.

75 reps total. Do sets of 10–15 with a strong plank and powerful hip snap. When form breaks or grip fades, put the bell down and rest. Keep a running count. Get to 75 however it takes.

The Rules
Never Miss. Never Grind.
Show up every session. Never miss a rep. Leave 2–3 in the tank every set. If you're questioning whether you'll make the rep, the weight is too heavy.
75 Real Swings
Dan John tested 250, 125, and finally settled on 75 reps of real swings per session. Not EMOM, not timed — just 75 quality reps. Sets of 10–15, rest when needed, stop at 75.
Carries: Never Repeat
The carry rotates every session across four variations: Farmer, Suitcase, Waiter, Rack. Never repeat the same carry session. Change the load or distance each time too.
Add Load When It Feels Easy
Wait until a weight feels genuinely easy for two consecutive sessions before adding load. OHP/Deadlift: +2.5–5kg. Pull-ups: add belt weight. Patience is the mechanism.
